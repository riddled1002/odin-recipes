Honey Biscuits

Description

These biscuits are decadent with a slight touch of sweetness from the honey.

Serve them with butter, jelly, jam, or more honey.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup cold buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons honey
